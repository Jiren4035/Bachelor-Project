Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple. Just click on the "Register" button at the top right corner of the homepage and fill in the required details.


Can I use ScanmyLog on multiple devices?

Yes, your ScanmyLog account is accessible on any compatible device, provided you log in with your credentials.


What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.


How does real-time monitoring work?

Our real-time monitoring system continuously analyzes your sever's and clients's activity to detect and alert you of any unusual behavior as it happens. This enables immediate response to potential threats, ensuring the security and integrity of your system.


Do the graphs update in real time?

Yes, our analytics dashboard features graphs that update in real time. This provides you with the most current view of your system's performance and security posture, allowing for timely decision-making.


Can the server collect its own logs?

Yes, the log collector is designed to collect logs from various sources, including its own operational logs. This self-monitoring capability ensures comprehensive visibility into the system's functionality and performance.


Can I send multiple alerts to multiple accounts?

Yes, our alert management system allows you to configure alerts to be sent to multiple accounts. This ensures that all relevant parties are promptly informed about critical events, enabling a coordinated response.
