Privacy Policy for ScanmyLog

ScanmyLog provides a leading security information and event management (‘SIEM’) solution for the automated collection, processing, and analysis of security event data. ScanmyLog collects user, host, and network data and utilizes both machine learning and deterministic detection techniques to gain seamless visibility, reducing the dwell time of threats that live outside the perimeter.
