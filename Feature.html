ScanmyLog Features

System Monitoring

ScanmyLog's System Monitoring is the cornerstone of our cybersecurity suite, providing continuous surveillance to ensure the peak performance of your critical system operations. This robust feature allows for real-time observation and analysis, ensuring that all components of your system are functioning as expected and that any anomalies are detected promptly. With our advanced monitoring tools, you can rest assured that your infrastructure remains secure and efficient, minimizing downtime and maintaining business continuity.

Analytics Dashboard

ScanmyLog's Analytics Dashboard stands out as a central feature of our platform, designed to cater expertly to the dynamic needs of modern enterprises. This highly customizable dashboard allows users to tailor the layout and the metrics displayed according to their specific requirements, ensuring that relevant data is always front and center. With advanced filtering options, users can effortlessly drill down into the data, isolating and examining specific segments or trends without being overwhelmed by the entirety of the data set. Additionally, the dashboard supports comprehensive export capabilities, enabling users to seamlessly download and share reports in various formats, facilitating easy dissemination and offline analysis. Most importantly, the dashboard showcases collected data in an expert manner by employing sophisticated visualization tools that transform complex datasets into clear, insightful visual representations. These visualizations aid in making informed decisions quickly, providing a strategic edge in operational and strategic planning.

Event Help

ScanmyLog's Event Help Page serves as a comprehensive guide designed to enhance users' understanding of various system events and their potential implications in cybersecurity attacks. This detailed resource breaks down each event type logged by our system, explaining its significance and how it might be exploited by attackers to compromise system integrity. By elucidating the connection between specific events and common attack vectors, users gain a deeper insight into the security landscape and learn to recognize early signs of malicious activity. In addition to detailed event descriptions, the page integrates a responsive chatbot, designed to provide real-time assistance. Users can interact with the chatbot by asking specific questions about events, seeking advice on interpreting unusual patterns, or querying potential security threats associated with observed activities. The chatbot, equipped with advanced AI, can also guide users through the process of setting up custom alerts for certain types of events, ensuring they are promptly informed of potentially hazardous activities.

Filter

ScanmyLog's Filter Page is engineered to streamline the management and analysis of extensive data logs collected by our system. This powerful tool enables users to filter and view all types of data, from routine operational logs to critical security events, facilitating a focused approach to data examination. With intuitive filtering options, users can easily specify criteria such as date ranges, event types, severity levels, and more, allowing for a tailored view that highlights the most relevant information. Once filtered, the data is displayed in an organized and accessible format, presenting users with a clear overview of the events that matter most. The page supports dynamic interactions with the data, including sorting and additional filtering within the displayed results, ensuring that users can drill down to the finest details as needed. In addition to viewing capabilities, the Filter Page offers robust export features. Users can export the filtered data into various formats such as CSV, PDF, or JSON, accommodating a range of uses from further offline analysis to compliance reporting. Moreover, the page includes options for users to send these reports directly to colleagues or stakeholders via email, promoting efficient communication and collaboration.

Alert

ScanmyLog's Alert Page is meticulously designed to empower users with the ability to proactively manage and respond to potential security threats through customized alerts. This intuitive platform allows users to create personalized alert conditions based on specific criteria such as event types, severity, or detected anomalies. Each alert can be finely tuned to match the user’s operational requirements and sensitivity preferences, ensuring that notifications are both relevant and timely. Key features of the Alert Page include the flexibility to set the frequency of alerts, allowing users to choose how often they receive updates - whether it’s instant notifications for critical issues or periodic summaries for ongoing monitoring. The sensitivity settings further refine the alert system, enabling users to adjust the thresholds that trigger these notifications, which helps in reducing false positives and focusing on genuine threats. Additionally, users can specify the destination email addresses for where alerts should be sent, facilitating direct communication to personal mailboxes or departmental groups. This feature ensures that the right personnel are alerted immediately, enhancing the ability to take swift and informed action. The Alert Page also integrates seamlessly with existing email systems, providing a reliable and efficient way to keep stakeholders informed. With these customizable options, the page is not just a tool for monitoring—it's a crucial part of a comprehensive security strategy, offering peace of mind and enhanced protective measures for any organization.

Case

ScanmyLog's Our Case Management Page is a pivotal tool designed to enhance organizational oversight and facilitate detailed audits by allowing users to curate and group suspicious events into cases. This feature-rich platform empowers users to handpick specific incidents that warrant further examination and compile them into structured cases. By organizing related events in this manner, it simplifies the task of tracking potential security threats and coordinating investigative efforts. The interface of the Case Management Page is intuitively designed to ensure ease of use, allowing users to select events based on various criteria such as time frames, severity, or type of anomaly. Once selected, these events can be added to new or existing cases with just a few clicks. Each case can be customized with unique identifiers and descriptions, making it straightforward to retrieve and review at any time, which is crucial for ongoing audits or when preparing compliance reports. Furthermore, this page includes robust search and filter tools to navigate through cases and events efficiently. It supports a workflow where users can update the status of each case, add notes, and even attach relevant documents or evidence, thereby maintaining a comprehensive record that is essential for thorough analysis and reporting.

UEBA

ScanmyLog's state-of-the-art User and Entity Behavior Analytics (UEBA) system leverages the cutting-edge Isolation Forest AI algorithm, renowned for its exceptional ability to detect anomalies in data, especially in identifying rare items, events, or observations which raise suspicions by differing significantly from the majority. This AI-driven approach enables our system to efficiently parse and analyze vast volumes of log data, pinpointing potential security threats by highlighting activities that deviate from established patterns of normal behavior. The UEBA system is equipped with sophisticated filtering tools that allow security analysts to refine and focus their review on the most relevant data points. Users can apply various filters such as date ranges, event categories, or risk levels to manage and navigate through the data more effectively. This capability ensures that analysts can quickly isolate and address the most pressing concerns without being overwhelmed by the volume of collected data. Additionally, our UEBA system features a dynamic dashboard that provides comprehensive data visualization tools. These tools transform complex datasets into intuitive and easy-to-understand visual formats, such as graphs, heat maps, and timelines. The dashboard not only enhances the visual appeal but significantly improves the interpretability of the data, allowing users to grasp complicated patterns and trends at a glance. This visual representation is crucial for prompt and informed decision-making in fast-paced security environments. Through the integration of the Isolation Forest algorithm with advanced filtering and visualization technologies, our UEBA system delivers a powerful solution for monitoring user and entity behaviors. It not only detects sophisticated insider threats and targeted attacks but also enhances the overall security posture by providing a clearer, more actionable overview of the network's activity landscape.

UEBA

ScanmyLog's Notification Page serves as a central hub for all communications generated by the User and Entity Behavior Analytics (UEBA) system, as well as alerts and updates from the alert and case management features. This comprehensive page is meticulously designed to ensure users have instant access to all relevant security notifications, enabling them to stay informed and react swiftly to any potential threats or required actions. The layout of the Notification Page is user-friendly and intuitive, displaying notifications in a clear, chronological list. Each notification is accompanied by a timestamp and a brief description, providing users with a quick overview of the event without needing to delve into detailed logs immediately. This setup allows for rapid assessment of the urgency and relevance of each alert or case update. Users can customize their notification settings to filter out routine messages and only receive alerts that meet specific criteria, such as severity levels or categories pertinent to their responsibilities. This customization helps in managing the flow of information and ensures that users are not overwhelmed by less critical notifications. Additionally, the page includes advanced sorting and filtering tools that allow users to organize their notifications based on various parameters such as date, status, or type. This functionality is particularly useful in busy operational environments where prioritization and quick access to the most critical alerts can significantly impact response times and outcomes.
